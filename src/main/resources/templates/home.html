Place your pet in the shade or air conditioning and apply cool—not cold—water to reduce the animal’s core body temperature. Never leave your dog in the car — never. Even on a pleasant day, temperatures in a parked car with the windows rolled down can exceed 38 degrees C within 10 minutes. Summer is the high season for fleas and ticks of all kinds, and the appropriate application of veterinarian-recommended tick medication can help keep your pet free from these pests.
